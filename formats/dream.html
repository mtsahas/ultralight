dream journal

The concept of a dream journal is not new, but many don't keep one. I think dreams are really interesting to talk about and analyze. This format can encourage people to record and share their dreams every morning.

  1. Wake up and take a moment to remember what you dreamed about.
  2. Write down the day and the dream you had.
  3. Publish it.
  4. Discuss.
